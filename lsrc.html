Law Students’ Representative Council (LSRC)

The legislative arm of the Law Students’ Society responsible for representation, policy direction and accountability within the LSS.

The Law Students’ Representative Council (LSRC) serves as the voice of Law students within the Society. Members of the Council are elected from different constituencies to deliberate on student-centred issues, pass resolutions, and provide oversight over the Executive Council of the LSS.

Through regular sittings, committee work and constituency engagement, the LSRC ensures that the concerns of students are heard and addressed in line with the Constitution of the Society and the values of Joseph Ayo Babalola University.

Mandate of the LSRC

  • Represent the interests of Law students across all levels.
  • Debate and pass resolutions on academic and welfare issues.
  • Exercise oversight over the LSS Executive Council.
  • Promote accountability, transparency and due process.

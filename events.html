Events & Activities

Moots, seminars, workshops and community outreach programmes organised by the Law Students’ Society.

12 February · Moot Court Hall

Inter-Faculty Moot Court Competition

Hosted by Moot & Mock Committee, bench constituted by the LSS Judicial Council.

Teams argue a simulated appellate case before a panel of practitioners, faculty members and student judges.

5 March · Online (Zoom)

Seminar: Pathways into Corporate & Commercial Practice

Virtual · LSS Career Development Desk

A conversation with alumni and practitioners sharing practical advice on internships, pupillage and early-career development.

16 March · Faculty Lecture Room 2

Legal Writing & Drafting Workshop

Facilitated by the LSS Academic Committee

Focused on structuring arguments, drafting opinions and improving the clarity and precision of legal writing.

30 March · Off-Campus Community

Community Legal Awareness & Rights Clinic

LSS Social Responsibility & Welfare Desk

Students engage community members on basic rights, contracts, tenancy and simple dispute resolution options.

10 April · Moot Court Hall

First-Year Introductory Moot & Mock Session

LSS Moot & Mock Trial Committee

A beginner-friendly session introducing first-year students to mooting, mock trial etiquette and courtroom procedure.

20 April · JABU Auditorium

Public Lecture: Law, Governance & Ethics in Nigeria

Joint programme with the Faculty of Law

Speakers explore the relationship between legal frameworks, public policy and ethical leadership in governance.

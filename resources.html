Resources by Level

100 Level

Includes courses such as Communication in English, Legal Method, Introduction to Psychology, Nigerian Constitutional Development, Introduction to International Law and more.

View 100 Level Materials

200 Level

Covers Nigerian Legal System I & II, Law of Contracts I & II, Constitutional Law I & II, History & Philosophy of Science, plus electives like Administrative Law, Human Rights and Labour Law.

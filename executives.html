Organisational Structure

A simple overview of how the Law Students’ Society is structured.

  • General Body of Law Students
  • Central Executive Council (CEC)
    • President
    • Vice President
    • General Secretary & Assistant General Secretary
    • Treasurer & Financial Secretary
    • Public Relations Officer
    • Welfare, Academic and other Directors/Officers
  • Law Students’ Representative Council (LSRC)
    • Class-appointed members representing each level of the Faculty
  • LSS Judicial Council
    • Student Justices/Panel Members constituted in line with the Constitution
  • Committees
    • Moot & Mock Trial Committee
    • Academic Committee
    • Welfare & Socials Committee
    • Other standing or ad hoc committees as required
